About Us

What is My Study Guide?

Welcome to My Study Guide! It is a free resources website. We are dedicated to providing a wide range of educational materials and resources to support school learners. My Study Guide is an educational website offering free resources for students for classes 10th, 11th and 12th.
It was developed with an intension to provide quality content for anyone to access and ace in their studies. We believe that education should be inclusive and available to everyone, regardless of financial constraints. The course curriculum is based as per the CBSE syllabus (The deleted portion of batch 2020 is also included). It can be accessed Anytime, Anywhere and by Anyone. You just need a stable internet connection and a smart device. That's it!!!
We have designed our website with user-friendly navigation in mind. You can easily browse through different subjects, grade levels, or specific topics to find the resources you need.

Our Services

  • Extensive Collection : We offer an extensive collection of free educational resources across various subjects and disciplines.
  • Comprehensive Subjects : Our resources cover a wide range of subjects, including math, science, social sciences and computer science.
  • Quality Assurance : We are committed to providing high-quality educational resources. Our team carefully curates and reviews each resource to ensure accuracy, relevance, and educational value.
  • Reliable Content :We aim to deliver content that is informative, engaging, and reliable.
  • Regular Updates : We are continuously expanding and updating our collection of resources. Our team is dedicated to adding new materials, incorporating user feedback, and staying up-to-date with the latest educational trends.
  • Accessible and Free : Our commitment to education extends to making resources accessible to all. All the resources on our website are completely free to access.
